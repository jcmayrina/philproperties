SM Development Corporation

Condominium and House and Lot Developer

Every single day, SM touches the lives of millions of people through its stores, malls, banks, hotels and leisure facilities. Filipinos can now live in style, comfort, and convenience with SMDC. With quality amenities and top-notch services, SMDC has the makings of your next dream home. SM Development Corporation commits itself to provide access to luxurious urban living through vertical villages perfectly integrated with a commercial retail environment, giving its residents access to a truly cosmopolitan lifestyle. Properties of SM Development Corporation are strategically situated in key areas across Metro Manila specifically the Central Business Districts of Mall of Asia Complex, Makati, Ortigas, Taguig, Quezon City, Manila, Pasay, Parañaque and Las Piñas. They can also be easily accessed from neighboring provinces like Cavite, Bulacan and Pampanga.
